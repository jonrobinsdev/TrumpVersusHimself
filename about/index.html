Trump vs. Himself

About Archive

This website was created for purposes of lampooning but to also expose hypocrisies coming out of the current administration by using a collection of tweets from the President himself. The idea was also in part to create a more visual and readable format based off of content from communities like the /r/TrumpCriticizesTrump subreddit, articles such as this one from The Washington Post, and many more places around the internet.

If you have any suggestions or feedback about the site, feel free to contact me at trumpversushimself@gmail.com.

Lastly, please consider making a donation to organizations like the ACLU or subscribing to hard-working news organizations such as The Washington Post, The Wall Street Journal, or The New York Times. Thank you.
